# 🤖 FRIDAY AI

FRIDAY is your **offline AI desktop assistant** inspired by Jarvis from Iron Man. It uses Text-to-Speech (TTS), Speech Recognition, and Automation tools to execute commands efficiently on your local machine without needing constant internet.

---

## ✨ **Features**

- 🔊 **Natural Speech Responses** (TTS)
- 🎙️ **Voice Command Recognition**
- 💻 **Open Applications & Websites**
- 🔍 **Perform Local Searches**
- 📝 **Custom Command Automation**
- 🛡️ **Offline & Private Operation**
